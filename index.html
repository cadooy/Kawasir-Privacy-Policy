Privacy Policy for Kawasir

Last Updated: December 2, 2024

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share your information when you use the Kawasir app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring full compliance with applicable laws such as GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, CalOPPA, and COPPA.

1. Definitions

User: Any individual using the app.

Personal Data: Any information that can identify a user.

App: Refers to the Kawasir app.

Services: Includes video streaming (such as MP4, M3U8) and prayer time features.

2. What Data Do We Collect?

A. Automatically Collected Data

- Device Information: Includes device type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.

- Location Data: Only collected to improve the accuracy of prayer times, with your consent.

B. Data We Do Not Collect

- The app does not require account registration, and therefore, no personal information like name, email, or phone number is collected.

- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. How Do We Use Your Data?

A. To Improve Our Services

- Provide personalized experiences (such as accurate prayer times based on your location).

- Improve app performance based on usage patterns.

B. Advertising

- Ads are served through Google AdMob, which may use your data to enhance ad targeting.

- You can view Google's privacy policy here.

4. Your Rights

A. GDPR Compliance

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Correct your data.
  • Object to data processing.
  • Request data deletion ("Right to be Forgotten").

B. CCPA/CPRA Compliance

For California users:

  • You have the right to know the data we collect.
  • Request data deletion.
  • Opt out of the sale of personal data (we do not sell your data).

C. CalOPPA Compliance

- You can use the app without creating an account.

- Any changes to this policy will be notified through an in-app notice.

D. COPPA Compliance

- We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

- If we discover such data collection, we will delete it immediately.

5. How Do We Protect Your Data?

- We implement high standards of security to protect your data.

- While no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, we follow industry best practices to safeguard your information.

6. Data Deletion

- You can request the deletion of your data at any time through the app's settings.

- We may retain some data for legal compliance purposes.

7. Links to External Websites

The app may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Policy Updates

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

- Users will be notified of any changes through an in-app notification.
